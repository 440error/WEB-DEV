1- INTER MILAN -> Inter Milan, commonly known as just Inter, is an Italian professional football club based in Milan, Italy. The club was founded in 1908 and has a long and successful history in Italian and European football. Inter Milan is one of the most successful clubs in Italy, having won 19 Serie A titles, 7 Coppa Italia titles, 5 Supercoppa Italiana titles, and 3 UEFA Champions League titles. The club has also won the UEFA Cup (now known as the Europa League) three times and the Intercontinental Cup twice.

2- AC MILAN -> AC Milan, also known simply as Milan or Rossoneri (meaning "Red and Blacks" in Italian), is an Italian professional football club based in Milan, Italy. The club was founded in 1899 and is one of the oldest and most successful clubs in Italian and European football. AC Milan has won 18 Serie A titles, 5 Coppa Italia titles, and 7 Supercoppa Italiana titles, making it one of the most successful clubs in Italian football history. The club has also won 7 UEFA Champions League titles, tied for second-most all-time with Liverpool FC, and has won the UEFA Super Cup five times. The club has had many legendary players over the years, including Paolo Maldini, Franco Baresi, Marco van Basten, Ruud Gullit, Andriy Shevchenko, Kaka, and many others.

3- REAL MADRID -> Real Madrid is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. The club was founded in 1902 and has established itself as one of the most successful and popular football clubs in the world. Real Madrid has won a record 14 UEFA Champions League titles, as well as numerous domestic titles, including 34 La Liga titles, 19 Copa del Rey titles, and 11 Supercopa de España titles. The club has also won 5 FIFA Club World Cup titles and 3 Intercontinental Cups. Real Madrid has had numerous legendary players throughout its history, including Alfredo Di Stefano, Ferenc Puskas, Francisco Gento, Emilio Butragueño, Raul, Cristiano Ronaldo, and many others. The club is also known for its "Galacticos" era in the early 2000s, during which it signed some of the world's most expensive and high-profile players, including Ronaldo, Zinedine Zidane, Luis Figo, and David Beckham

4-MANCHESTER CITY -> Manchester City, also known as Man City, is an English professional football club based in Manchester, UK. The club was founded in 1880 and has since established itself as one of the most successful clubs in English football. Man City has won 7 English Premier League titles, 7 FA Cup titles, and 7 EFL Cup titles, making it one of the most successful clubs in recent years. The club has also won one UEFA Cup Winners' Cup and one UEFA Intertoto Cup. The team plays its home matches at the Etihad Stadium, which has a seating capacity of over 55,000 and is one of the most modern and advanced football stadiums in the world. Man City is known for its sky-blue home kit and its passionate fanbase. In recent years, Man City has undergone a transformation under the ownership of the Abu Dhabi United Group, with significant investment in players and infrastructure leading to a period of sustained success. The club has had numerous star players, including Sergio Aguero, Vincent Kompany, Kevin De Bruyne,Halland and Raheem Sterling.
